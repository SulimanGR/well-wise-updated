BMR and Nutrition References

BMR Calculation: The code uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, a trusted formula for calculating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). This formula is widely used in clinical and fitness fields. Source: American Journal of Clinical Nutrition and Healthline .

Caloric Adjustments for Weight Goals: Adjusting calories by 500 per day for weight gain or loss is a common guideline for safe weight management. Source: CDC and NIDDK .

Protein and Macronutrient Distribution: The protein values (1.6-2.2 g/kg) are recommended by the International Society of Sports Nutrition (ISSN) and help support specific goals like weight loss or maintenance. Source: Journal of the International Society of Sports Nutrition .
